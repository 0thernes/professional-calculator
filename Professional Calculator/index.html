Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Digits 0 through 9 enter numbers.
  • Plus, minus, asterisk, slash for operators.
  • Enter or equals to calculate.
  • Backspace deletes the last digit.
  • Escape clears the calculator.
  • Control Z or Control U undoes the last action.
  • Control Y or Control Shift Z redoes.
  • Control N toggles negative.
